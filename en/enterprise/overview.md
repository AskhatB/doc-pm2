PM2 Enterprise is an advanced Node.js toolset that is convenient for high scale or critical Node.js services.

It answers to the need of companies willing to bet a lot on Node.js and be confident on their technology switch.

It includes not only advanced features like Smart Anomaly Detection, Automatic Remediation System and more, but also includes premium support in direct communication with our engineers, expert Node.js trainings and custom features.

## Auto Remediation System

<br/>
<p align="center">
    <img width="70%" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/img/enterprise/alert_global.png" alt="alert feature">
</p>

When plugging custom actions to your Node.js application, you will be able to trigger theses alert on certain threshold and be able to apply pre emptive correctional actions to make your application automatically repair it self.

It can be also used as a classical threshold based alerting system to send notification and alert to your team via email/slack or webhooks.

## Log Storage System

<br>
<p align="center">
    <img width="90%" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/img/enterprise/logs.png" alt="logs feature">
</p>

Our log storage system permits to keep your applications logs up to 1year in a easily searchable interface.


## On-premise Installation

We offer different way of delivering PM2 Enterprise via easily installable on-premise system or dedicated/managed instances.
